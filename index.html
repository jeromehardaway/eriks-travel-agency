Vacation Plan For Family Of Four: Nashville

by Erik Billings

With a vast multidimensional history that includes the Civil War, shaping music history, and unique culinary tastes such as Hot Chicken and cotton candy Nashville TN is the destination that will give your family high-voltage memories to cherish for decades

Travel

Get 40 mpg highway and 30 mpg city with a rental of the stylish 2019 Volkswagen Jetta.

Places To Go

Hear and feel the culture resonate through you with stay at the Gaylord Opryland Resort + SoundWaves water park and music experience

Landmarks

Take a moment to pause your trip on the way to Nashville to gauge at the awe-inspiring Land Between The Lakes, the largest in-land peninsula in the United States.

Wildlife

Enjoy nature when you spot these native Tennessean wildlife species: Coopers Hawk, Nine Banded Armadillo, Deer, Owls, and Red Fox.
